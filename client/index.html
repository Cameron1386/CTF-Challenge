Panda Proxy Agency

You are an intern at the Panda Proxy Agency, an elite, covert cybersecurity firm. Agents are known only by their panda code names. Your mentor, Agent Pao Pao, assigns you to track down and disrupt criminal cyber gangs who are careless but dangerous. Using clues, puzzles, and coding, you’ll peel back their operations and uncover their plans.

The Golden Talon

The Golden Talon is a flashy, sloppy cybergang that loves bragging online. They post “mini puzzles” and weak web apps for fun. This arc is meant to get you warmed up with simple challenges.
