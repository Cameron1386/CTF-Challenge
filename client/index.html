What is Pandemonium??

Learn Through a Story

Pandemonium is a story-based cybersecurity game where students become interns at the Panda Proxy Agency. Players will solve mysteries, follow clues, and uncover cybercriminal plots—one puzzle at a time.

Real Skills, Beginner-Friendly

Students practice real cybersecurity skills like spotting hidden messages, breaking simple ciphers, exploring websites safely, and writing beginner code. No prior experience required.

Safe & Age-Appropriate

Pandemonium is built specifically for middle and elementary school students. All challenges are simulated, ethical, and guided—teaching how cyberattacks work so students learn how to defend against them.
